Program rules

How Emissio is run

Short version: contribute honestly, one account per person, and the ledger pays out at launch. The details below are the rules reviewers apply.

Accounts

  • One account per person. Networks of accounts farming the same rewards are disqualified and their balances voided.
  • You are responsible for the email and password on your account and for the address you submit. Rewards sent to the address on file at launch are considered delivered.

Rewards

  • Rewards are denominated in the Sequence token (SEQ) and paid on the Sequentia mainnet at launch, to the mainnet address on your account.
  • Every reward requires review. Reviewers approve evidence that clearly shows you completed the task; fabricated or recycled evidence forfeits the reward and can void the account.
  • Capped tasks pay the first accounts whose evidence is approved, in review order, until the cap is reached.
  • Competition prizes and security awards are decided by the Sequentia team. Those decisions are final.
  • The launch price of 0.375 USD per SEQ is the initial mainnet price used for reference throughout this site. It is not a guarantee of any market price after launch.

Security reports

  • Reports are received privately and handled by the security review team. Do not disclose publicly before a fix ships.
  • The first clear report of an issue is rewarded. Later reports of the same issue are marked duplicates.
  • Test only against your own nodes and wallets or the public testnet, and never against other users.

Account verification

  • You can link a Telegram, X and Reddit account to your profile, {{seq 15}} SEQ each. The linked account must be at least two years old and must show your Emissio account code in its bio or public description at review time.
  • A social account can vouch for one Emissio account, ever. Verification is optional, but linked accounts are strong evidence of one-person-one-account, and unverified accounts get closer scrutiny before large payouts.
  • No identity documents are ever requested. Verification means proving control of an aged public handle, nothing more.
  • To detect farming, we record the IP address an account registers from and compare it across accounts, alongside transaction evidence and referral patterns.

Referrals

  • Your referral link is on your account page. A referral pays {{seq 10}} SEQ to each side once both accounts have earned at least {{seq 50}} SEQ from tasks, competitions or security reports. Referral and pre-sale credits do not count toward that threshold.
  • Referral rewards are paid for at most 20 referrals per account.
  • Each on-chain transaction counts as evidence for one account only, and networks of accounts referring each other to farm rewards are disqualified under the one-account rule.

Payout

  • Before launch, a snapshot is taken of every account's balance and address. Balances are paid to those addresses in the launch allocation.
  • Accounts without a valid mainnet address at the snapshot keep their balance reserved for twelve months after launch. Add an address in that window and the balance is paid in the next distribution; after the window, unclaimed balances return to the program pool.
  • Testnet assets, including tSEQ, have no value and are not what this program pays. Emissio balances are the only record that counts.

Pre-sales

  • Emissio may occasionally offer short pre-sale windows in which registered account holders can buy Sequence tokens below the launch price. Windows are announced in advance, are strictly limited in size and duration, and may be restricted by jurisdiction.
  • Participation is always optional and never affects reward eligibility.

Changes

The Sequentia team may adjust rewards, caps, and rules for future contributions as the program grows. Credits already entered in your ledger are not reduced by rule changes.
