The pool is the amount reserved for this program. Amounts are credited only after human review of your evidence.

How it works

1. Contribute

Complete tasks on the Sequentia testnet, enter competitions, or report vulnerabilities. Most tasks take minutes and produce on-chain evidence: a transaction id.

2. Submit evidence

Submit the txid from your account. We check it against the testnet, a reviewer approves it, and the entry appears in your ledger. Each transaction counts for one account only.

3. Receive at launch

Add a mainnet address to your account (the guide shows how to generate one safely). At mainnet launch your full balance is paid to that address.

Security rewards

Found a bug or a vulnerability in the node, the wallets, or the infrastructure? Report it privately. Accepted reports pay from 250 SEQ up to {{seq .Data.TierMax}} SEQ for consensus-breaking or funds-loss issues. Severity tiers and rules.

Referrals

Bring someone in with the referral link on your account page. Once you have each earned at least 50 SEQ from tasks, competitions or security reports, you both receive 10 SEQ, automatically. Paid for up to 20 referrals per account.

Pre-sale windows

From time to time, Emissio may open short pre-sale windows in which account holders can buy Sequence tokens below the 0.375 USD launch price. Windows are small, brief, and announced in advance to registered accounts. Nothing to do now: having an account is what makes you eligible to be notified.
