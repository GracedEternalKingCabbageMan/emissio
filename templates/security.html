Security rewards

Break it on the testnet, not at launch

Report vulnerabilities in the Sequentia node, the wallets, SeqDEX, SeqLN, or the project infrastructure. Reports are private: only you and the review team can see them. Accepted reports are credited in SEQ according to severity, and a proposed fix raises the award within its tier.

Amounts are the reference award for a clear, reproducible report in that tier. The review team sets the final award. The first reporter of an issue is the one rewarded; later reports of the same issue are marked duplicates.

Ground rules

  • Test against your own nodes and wallets, or the public testnet. Never against other users or their funds.
  • No denial of service against shared infrastructure beyond the minimum needed to prove the issue.
  • Report privately here first. Public disclosure before a fix ships forfeits the reward.
  • Include steps to reproduce. A working proof of concept and a proposed fix both raise the award.
